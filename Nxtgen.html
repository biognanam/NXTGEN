How It Works

From Sample to Clinical Insight

1

Sample Collection

Blood, tissue, or swab collected at your hospital or our partner collection centres across Salem.

2

DNA Extraction & QC

ISO-compliant nucleic acid extraction with quality control checkpoints before library preparation.

3

NGS Sequencing

High-throughput sequencing on Illumina platforms via our certified partner laboratory network.

4

Bioinformatics Analysis

GATK / QIIME2 pipelines process raw FASTQ data through variant calling, annotation, and filtering.

5

Clinical Report

Clinician-ready report with variant interpretation, therapeutic implications, and actionable recommendations in 3–5 days.
